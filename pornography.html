Pornography

Pornography (also shortened to porn or pr0n) is visual material, such as streaming video, that illustrates sexual activity.

Pro-tip: Use your web browser's private browsing feature.

There is nothing wrong when consenting adults make porn. Be aware that the porn stars are portraying fictional situations. They may also have prepared their bodies for certain sexual acts prior to being filmed (see also: anal sex). Before recreating (Internet-inspired) sex acts with your partner, always ask for your partner's permission. If they say no, then respect that decision.

Never coerce or manipulate someone into doing something that they are not comfortable doing. Non-consensual sex is rape, punishable by jail time. You may withdraw consent anytime. False accusations of rape are equally as immoral and unethical as rape.
